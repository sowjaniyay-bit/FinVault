About FinVault

FinVault — Smart Finance Manager
A smart, beautiful personal finance manager built for Indian users.
Track every rupee. Forecast your future. Get AI-powered nudges and smart alerts.
💳
Transaction Tracking
Auto-categorises Indian merchants — Zomato, Swiggy, Flipkart, UPI, NEFT and more across 11 categories.
📊
Budget Limits
Set ₹ limits per category. Progress bars turn amber then red as you approach limits.
📈
14-Day Forecast
Predicts your balance for next 14 days using your income and spending trends.
🔔
AI Nudges
Proactive smart alerts based on your spending — budget alerts, bill reminders, salary tips.
🏦
Indian Banks
Sample data for SBI, HDFC, ICICI, Axis, Kotak, PNB, BOB with realistic UPI and EMI transactions.
🔔
Smart Alerts
Upcoming bill reminders, overspend warnings, subscription cost alerts, and spending reduction tips.
